字体简介
《小黑滚滚体》于3月25日正式开源发布，面向全球完全永久免费可商用，可修改、编辑、二次创作。《小黑滚滚体》，秉承自由的理念，浑厚可爱，运用圆滚动后的痕迹形成正负形的形式，根据书写逻辑融合几何图形运动去表达，用在标题、logo文字中效果极佳。

Font introduction
"Little Black Rolling Body" was officially released as open source on March 25. It is completely free and commercially available for the world, and can be modified, edited, and re-created. "Little Black Rolling Body" adheres to the concept of freedom, is simple and cute, uses the traces of the circle to form positive and negative forms, and integrates geometric movement to express according to the writing logic. It is used in titles and logos to great effect.
